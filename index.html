Why CleanCity Hub?

This web app solves the real-world problem of waste mismanagement in our district. Residents can report issues instantly, schedule pickups, and join clean-ups — all in one place.

  • ✅ Built with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5 & JavaScript
  • ✅ Form validation prevents bad data
  • ✅ Simulated realistic data & social feeds
  • ✅ Dark theme with black, grey & orange accents
  • ✅ Scalable: Just change the district in the dropdown above
